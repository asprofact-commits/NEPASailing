Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Saronida Sailing, operated by Captain Theodoros as a sole operator based in Saronida, Attica, Greece (“we”, “us”, “our”), collects and uses your personal data when you visit saronidasailing.gr, contact us, or book a sailing trip. We take your privacy seriously and handle your information in line with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Greek law.

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal data to anyone. We collect only what we need to answer your enquiry and run your trip safely.

2. What data we collect

We only collect data you give us or that is needed to deliver a trip:

  • Contact details — your name, phone number, email and/or WhatsApp number.
  • Booking details — preferred dates, group size, names of guests, and any special requests (for example dietary needs, accessibility, or an occasion such as a birthday).
  • Communications — the messages you send us by WhatsApp, phone, email or social media.
  • Payment information — we may record that a deposit or payment was made and its amount. Card payments, where used, are processed by a third-party payment provider; we do not store full card numbers.
  • Website data — basic, mostly anonymous technical data such as device type and pages viewed (see Cookies below).

We do not knowingly collect data from children. Guests under 18 must be booked by a parent or guardian.

3. Why we use your data & legal basis

  • To respond to your enquiry and arrange your trip — legal basis: steps taken at your request prior to a contract, and performance of our contract with you.
  • For safety and seamanship — knowing guest numbers and any relevant needs lets us run the trip safely — legal basis: our legitimate interest and, where relevant, legal obligation.
  • To take payment and keep accounting records — legal basis: performance of contract and our legal obligations under Greek tax law.
  • To send you trip-related messages (confirmations, weather updates, meeting point) — legal basis: performance of contract.
  • To send occasional offers or news, only if you ask us to — legal basis: your consent, which you can withdraw at any time.

4. Who we share your data with

We keep your data within our own operation wherever possible. We only share it with:

  • Payment providers — to process a deposit or payment securely.
  • Messaging platforms — for example WhatsApp (Meta), when you choose to contact us through them, subject to their own privacy terms.
  • Authorities — such as the port authority or our accountant, where required by law or for safety.

We never sell your data or use it for third-party advertising.

5. How long we keep your data

We keep enquiry and booking data only as long as needed to serve you and to meet legal obligations — typically for the duration of the booking process and up to the period required by Greek tax and accounting law for financial records. Marketing consent is kept until you withdraw it. After that, your data is deleted or anonymised.

6. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

  • access the personal data we hold about you;
  • ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • ask us to delete your data (“right to be forgotten”), where applicable;
  • restrict or object to how we use your data;
  • request a copy of your data in a portable format;
  • withdraw consent at any time, where we rely on consent.

To exercise any of these, contact us using the details below. We will respond within one month.

7. Cookies & analytics

Our website uses only the cookies and scripts needed for it to work and, where enabled, privacy-respecting analytics to understand how the site is used (for example which pages are popular). These do not identify you personally. If we add advertising or tracking cookies in future, we will ask for your consent first through a cookie banner. You can also block or delete cookies in your browser settings.

8. How we protect your data

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to keep your data safe and limit access to it. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, but we take care to protect the limited information you share with us.

9. Contact & complaints

For any privacy question or request, email Captain Theodoros at info@saronidasailing.gr, or contact him on WhatsApp / phone at +30 697 299 6191.

If you believe we have not handled your data properly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Hellenic Data Protection Authority (Αρχή Προστασίας Δεδομένων Προσωπικού Χαρακτήρα) at www.dpa.gr.
